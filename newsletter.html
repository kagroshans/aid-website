Get the Note Screening Checklist

A 20-minute framework for evaluating any mortgage note before committing capital. Free for physicians.

What's in the Checklist

Before I buy any mortgage note, I run through a structured set of questions. Borrower profile, property location, lien position, payment history, servicer quality — each one matters.

This checklist is the tool I use to move from "interesting deal" to "yes" or "no" in under 20 minutes. It's not a guarantee. It's a process. And it's free.
